Why You Should Join UCF As An Undergraduate




Student Social Life UCF offers tons of social activities for all interests. There are all types of groups: political, exercise, hobbies, and more! See more information here!

Undergraduate Research Undergraduate research allows you to participate in the groundbreaking things going on in your field right now! More information here!

Fraternities & Sororities A wide range of Greek organizations will help to match you with people going for your same degree, and give you a home away from home! Check them out here!

Football & other Sports UCF has a wide range of competetive sports teams. Stadiums of every time house sporting events from around the country. Check out sports here!

Large Student Body UCF boasts a very large student population. There are over 60,000 students currently attending the university.

Internship Opportunities UCF is one of the best universities in the country to get a great internship that can later land you a career. More information here!

Huge Campus The UCF campus is very large and beautiful. The 1,415 acre campus is packed with things to do and see.

Based in Orlando UCF is within close range of Disney World and Universal Studios. The university hosts yearly free trips to Universal.

Free Medical Care UCF offers free medical and counseling services. There is dental care, psychiatric services, a pharmacy and more!
